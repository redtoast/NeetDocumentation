Meet NEET computers!

What is NEET?

NEET is a Minecraft mod for 1.21.1 (Fabric) that adds code executing computers to the world! These computers can actually run real code, inspired by ComputerCraft by DanTwoHundred and its modern fork CC:Tweaked by SquidDev. NEET attempts to improve the base idea of previous computer mods, and creates a system with vivid graphics, full user IO, and beginner-friendly features for players without a programming background to be able to interact with and enjoy computing in Minecraft!

But why a NEET computer?

NEET utilises full 384 x 288 graphics with 256 colors, and a programmatic display that renders directly on the block model! It also has full filesystem support including binary files, partitions, and live rewritable OS folders. As for implementation, network support may be coming soon and peripherals are fully implemented.

But what about development?

For full disclosure, NEET is only being continuously developed by one person (me). I'm also making the website, writing all the documentation, patching bugs, writing Lua programs, and in education.

I'm proud of how far this random idea I had to improve ComputerCraft has come, and how cool its become and all the progress I have made. It's unrealistic for me to make it faster than I do, and I can't focus on the mod all the time. Without help, progress won't be impressive, but I plan to keep the mod as high quality as I can. I will provide resources for people who like my idea. The mod will certainly receive more updates in the future, I'm not done with it yet.

I'm only one person, please be kind!
